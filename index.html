What we make

Quiet, dignified digital keepsakes for pet loss

HeirSong is an independent studio creating soft, trauma-informed templates and rituals for grieving pet parents. Everything is delivered as instant digital downloads — used the moment you need them, kept as long as you need them.

  • Eulogy & ceremony templates

    Fillable speech templates and at-home memorial service scripts — for the day someone asks you to speak, and the small ceremony you want to hold.

  • Pet grief journals

    Trauma-informed daily prompts grounded in the work of the Association for Pet Loss and Bereavement. For the twenty-eight days no one tells you about.

  • Anniversary & remembrance kits

    One-month, six-month, one-year rituals. The first holiday without them. The birthday that would have been.

  • Custom watercolor portraits

    Hand-styled keepsake portraits and tribute letters — the way you'll always picture them, made still. Available later in 2026.

If you're newly grieving

You are not alone, and there are people to call.

HeirSong is one small studio. These organizations are larger, free, and staffed by people trained for this. If you are in the rawest part of grief, please reach for them first.

  • APLB — Association for Pet Loss and Bereavement · aplb.org
  • Cornell Pet Loss Hotline — free phone support · vet.cornell.edu
  • Lap of Love — pet hospice and grief resources · lapoflove.com
