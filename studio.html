The Studio

Three quiet collections, made slowly, made with care.

HeirSong is a small studio. Each piece — printable templates, ceremony scripts, anniversary kits, tribute keepsakes — is designed to hold the weight of a difficult moment without rushing it. Most arrive as instant downloads. Some are made to order. Nothing is mass-produced or templated for volume.

The catalogue is organized into three collections, arranged loosely by who they're for and when they're for. Some pieces are available today. Others are opening in the coming weeks. A few are still being designed, with care, in our studio.

Below: a closer look at each collection.
