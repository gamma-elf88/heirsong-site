The Studio · Pet collection

For the companions we don't get to keep.

Soft, structured pieces for the day after, the first month, and every anniversary that follows.

The Pet collection is HeirSong's first home. Each piece below is designed to do one specific thing well — meet you in a particular moment of grief and give you something to do with your hands while the rest of you catches up.

Some pieces are available today. Others are in design. If anything below sounds like something you might need, leave your email and we'll let you know — quietly, with no pressure — when it's ready.
