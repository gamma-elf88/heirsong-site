The Studio · Family & Memorial

For the people whose absence reshapes a family.

This collection is in design. Below is what's coming, and how to be told — quietly — when each piece is ready.

HeirSong's Family & Memorial collection is being designed slowly, with the same editorial care given to the Pet collection. Nothing is purchasable yet. If anything below feels like something you might need, leave your email and we'll let you know — once, gently — when it ships.

The photo where they're still in it

A restored or reimagined family photograph that includes someone who has died. For the wedding they didn't make it to, the graduation that came after, the holiday that happened anyway. Hand-finished, never mass-generated.

Memorial Photograph Restoration

A faded photograph, brought back. Damaged corners repaired, colors gently revived, the moment preserved. For the photograph you've been meaning to get to for years.

Opening soon

Tribute Song

A custom song written from the memories you share — their voice, their phrases, the small things that made them who they were. Delivered as a recording you can keep and share.

Animated Tribute Card

A short animated card with a photograph, a written line, and quiet motion. For birthdays that come anyway, anniversaries that mark themselves, and the small acknowledgements between family members.
